3. HOW THE PROGRAM WORKS
The new Program process consists of four elements:

a. Program Sign Up: Program enrollment takes place on the Program homepage, where customer creates an account, selects user preferences and completes device identifying information. Upon enrollment, customer receives a program welcome email sent to the email address associated with their Lexmark.com user account.

b. Homepage: This serves as the tool for quick replenishment selection and reorder. The Program homepage is accessible via the Lexmark.com customer account and displays all eligible supplies.

c. Replenishment Notification: The new Program’s replenishment process is supported by a series of email reminders. By enrolling in the Program, customer agrees to receive emails from Lexmark.com for the purpose of the Program.  Replenishment orders receive a 15% discount (excluding the 105XL series) and free shipping. Inkjet cartridges must be purchased in any combination of two or more cartridges in order to be eligible for free shipping.

Customer must elect to save his or her credit card information in the account management settings in order to utilize the Program quick-reorder functionality.

There is no cost to join the Program.

Most supplies on the web are eligible for the Program.

Lexmark-branded Return Program supplies are available for purchase through the Program. (The counterpart to this option, regular cartridges, are excluded from this program.) Return Program supplies are sold at a special price subject to the restriction that the cartridge may be used only once. Following their initial use, you agree to return the empty toner cartridge to Lexmark for remanufacturing and/or recycling. The 105XL inkjet cartridge is also excluded from this program, in additional to the regular choice noted above.

A customer can choose to cancel their participation in the Program at any time.

OTHER THINGS YOU NEED TO KNOW

Replenishment orders will be sent within 3-8 business days of your online order transaction

Inkjet cartridges must be purchased in any combination of two or more cartridges in order to be eligible for free shipping.

Standard shipping is the only shipping option eligible for free shipping.

Customers will be notified by email when a replacement cartridge is shipped.
